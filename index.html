On the Psychological Basis of Enneagram tests - Enneagram Type 6

This section will continue to go into detail about the psychological basis of Enneagram tests specifically in relation to Enneagram Type 6 individuals. In Enneagram theory, which is considered by many to have never been a wholly scientifically-based theory, Type 6 individuals are those who are gonna give their all no matter what you or anyone say. In corporate situations, they climb up the ladder by being loyal to their friends and their colleagues. Those people are characterized by how they never gonna let everyone, including you, be attacked or put down. However, they can be very obstinate, never changing their mind once they have decided on something. They are gonna keep going even if everyone else decides to turn around, which means that they may not always be logical and calm. However, they will never desert you.
